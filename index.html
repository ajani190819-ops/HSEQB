Help & Tutorial

Getting Started

Open the sidebar menu and work through each section in order: Session → Players → Teams → Categories. Once set up, use the Record Answer panel to log answers in real time.

Sessions

Each match is a Session. Create a new one with a name, or load a previous one. Sessions sync across all connected devices automatically.

Players & Teams

Add players in the Players section. Then create teams in the Teams section and assign players to them. Only players assigned to a team appear as selectable in the Record Answer panel.

Recording Answers

Select a Player, then a Point Type, then a Category, then press Record Answer.

TypePointsNotes
Toss-up+10Standard correct answer
Bonus+10Follow-up bonus question
Power+15Early correct answer
Neg-5Incorrect interrupt — category optional

Analytics

Click Analytics in the header to view detailed player and category breakdowns, including bar charts, category performance, and full leaderboard.

Statistics & Rankings

Player rankings use tiebreakers: most Powers wins, then fewest Negs.

Export & Import

Export to CSV/Excel for spreadsheets, or JSON to back up and re-import a full session.

Visual Settings

Toggle Dark Mode, change Accent Color, or hide scrollbars. Saved to your device only.

Data Management

The Delete All Data button permanently removes all sessions. It requires an admin password. Export your data first!
