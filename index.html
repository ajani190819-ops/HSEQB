Help & Tutorial

```

Getting Started

Open the sidebar and work through each section in order: Session → Players → Teams → Categories. Once configured, use the Record Answer panel to log answers in real time. All data syncs automatically across connected devices via Firebase.

Sessions

Each match is a Session. Create a new one with a descriptive name, or use Load Session to switch to a previous match. From the Load Session screen you can also mark sessions as excluded from analytics — excluded sessions are dimmed and ignored in all Stats calculations.

Players & Teams

Add players in the Players section, then create teams in Teams and assign players to them. Only players assigned to a team are selectable when recording answers. Players added to the global player pool are available across all sessions.

Recording Answers

Select a Player, then a Point Type, then a Category (optional for Neg), then press Record Answer. Use the answer log to delete mistakes.

TypePointsNotes
Toss-up+10Standard correct answer on a toss-up
Bonus+10Follow-up bonus question after a toss-up
Power+15Correct answer before the power mark
Neg-5Incorrect interrupt — category is optional

Questions Heard Counter

The Questions Heard counter in the Record Answer panel tracks how many toss-up questions have been read. It increments automatically on every Toss-up, Power, or Neg answer (counting a Neg followed immediately by a Toss-up/Power as one question). Use the +/- buttons to correct it manually. This value powers the efficiency metrics in Stats.

Stats (Analytics)

Toggle to the Stats view using the switch in the header. Stats are aggregated across all valid (non-excluded) sessions and organized into four tabs:

  • Overview — session totals, category strengths & weaknesses, top performers
  • Players — ranked list of all players; click any row to expand their full breakdown including efficiency metrics
  • Categories — answer counts and point totals by subject area
  • Team Compositions — per-team stats for every session, titled by member names; click to expand efficiency data

Player Efficiency Metrics

Expand any player in the Players tab to see four metrics: Potential Points (total points if all toss-up wins earned bonuses), Production Rate (potential points per question heard), Buzz Volume (% of questions the player buzzed on), and Aggression (powers-to-negs ratio — 100% means powers with zero negs).

Rankings & Tiebreakers

Players are ranked by total points. Ties are broken first by most Powers, then by fewest Negs.

Export & Import

Export to CSV for use in spreadsheets, or JSON to back up and re-import a full session. Both options are in the Data section of the sidebar.

Visual Settings

Toggle Dark Mode, change the Accent Color, or hide scrollbars in the Visual Settings section. These preferences are saved to your device only and do not sync.
```
